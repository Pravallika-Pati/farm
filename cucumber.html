Benefits:

  • Putting them on your skin may help ease sunburn pain, swelling, and damaged skin.

Storage and uses:

  • Store cucumbers unpeeled in your refrigerator's crisper drawer.
  • Storing cucumbers below fifty degrees Fahrenheit can cause chilling injuries, such as mushy spots and faster spoilage.
