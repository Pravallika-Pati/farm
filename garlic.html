Garlic

Season :

March to June

Benefits:

  • Garlic Helps Boost Your Body’s Immune System
  • Garlic Helps Reduce High Blood Pressure
  • Garlic Can Help With Prevention of Cancer

Storage and uses:

  • The easiest way to store fresh garlic at home is at room temperature in mesh bags.
  • The ideal temperature to store garlic is about 60–65°F (15–18°C) in mild humidity.

Soil and Climate :

Garlic grows best in well-drained, moisture-retentive soil with pH between 6.0 and 7.0.

Temperature :

Garlic is best maintained at temperatures of 30 to 32 °F with low RH (60 to 70%).

Fertilizers Requirements :

The best garlic plant fertilizer will be high in nitrogen, those containing blood meal or a synthetic source of nitrogen.
