Onion

Season :

Annual crop

Benefits:

  • Onions are nutrient-dense, meaning they're low in calories but high in vitamins and minerals.
  • Onions contain antioxidants and compounds that fight inflammation, decrease triglycerides and reduce cholesterol levels

Soil:

well-drained soil with pH between 6.0 and 7.0, and high organic matter

Temperature :

Onions stored at a temperature near 0oC for 150 days with no rotting or sprouting occurring during this period.Storage below freezing is most suitable for onions high in soluble solids intended for processing. Onions can also be stored at high temperatures of 29.2 - 34.7oC.

Fertilizers Required:

A nitrogen-based fertilizer (ammonium sulfate or ammonium nitrate)
