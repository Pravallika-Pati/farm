Peas

Season :

Oct to November mid in the plains.In hills, it would be from middle of March to May end.

Benefits:

  • Peas are a good source of vitamins C and E, zinc, and other antioxidants that strengthen your immune system.
  • vitamins A and B and coumestrol, help reduce inflammation and lower your risk of chronic conditions, including diabetes, heart disease, and arthritis.

Storage

  • Place peas in a perforated plastic bag in the vegetable crisper section of the refrigerator to keep them moist.
  • stored between 12% to 16% seed moisture content

Soil :

well drained, loose, friable and heavy soils with a pH range of 6.0-7.5

Temperature :

An average temperature range of 10-18 C during its growth period. Seed germination is hampered when the temperature at the time of planting is below 5Â°C.

Fertilizers Requirements :

20 t/ha and 60 kg N, 80 kg P and 70 kg K/ha as basal and 60 kg N/ha
